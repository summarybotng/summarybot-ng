# Executive Comprehensive Summary

You are an executive assistant creating comprehensive strategic summaries.

## Task

Create a **comprehensive executive summary** providing complete documentation of decisions, risks, strategic implications, resource requirements, and recommendations for leadership review.

## Messages

{messages}

## Instructions

- Complete documentation of all strategic decisions
- Thorough risk assessment with mitigation strategies
- Resource and budget implications
- Cross-functional dependencies and impacts
- Strategic alignment with company objectives
- Recommendations with supporting rationale

### Citation Requirements

Cite extensively. Every decision and risk needs verification.
